This is my story about my journey to become a Frontend developer, where I share my experiences, challenges, and growth as I strive to establish myself in the crazy cyber world.

In 2006, after graduating from school, I began my journey in the IT industry and trying secured my first job. However, life took an unexpected turn when I decided to temporarily pause my career and embark on an adventure to Scotland. During this time, fate intervened, and I crossed paths with the remarkable person who would become my lifelong partner. Over the course of a few months, we forged a deep connection and together envisioned the life we wanted to build. While my wife pursued her aspirations to become a teacher, I took a brief hiatus from my professional pursuits. Now, as the stars align, it is my moment to return to my rightful place and reignite my passion for the world of Frontend development.
